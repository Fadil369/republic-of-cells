# Republic of Cells (جمهورية الخلايا)

A bilingual (Arabic/English) novel by Dr. Mohamed El Fadil, MD, narrating the human body as a republic of ~30 trillion citizens — cells that faithfully serve a whole they can never perceive or understand.

## Contents

- `THE-REPUBLIC-OF-CELLS-English-Complete-v2.pdf` — the English edition.
- `جمهورية الخلايا — الطبعة الكاملة.pdf` / `.html` — the complete Arabic edition.
- `Republic of Cells Audit Report.txt` — the raw, unedited export of a structural/literary/translation audit of the manuscript-in-progress, plus a reading companion, an original inspired-by short story, and a premium book layout template. Kept as-is for the record.
- `docs/AUDIT-REPORT.md` — a cleaned, de-duplicated, and reorganized edition of the audit above: completeness status, consistency/error findings, literary analysis, the Arabic translation audit, and a de-duplicated priority action list.
- `docs/DEVELOPMENT-ROADMAP.md` — the forward-looking companion: a fix-first checklist, a chapter-outline proposal for the underdeveloped Movement IX, new character concept sketches (the Antibody, the Neutrophil, the Mitochondrion, the Hepatocyte, the Podocyte, the Messenger Hormone), and structural/production ideas for future drafting sessions.
